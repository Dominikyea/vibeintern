Case Study

Touch Grass Campaign

We changed the Xverse app icon to include grass underneath the logo. Users discovered they were "touching grass" every time they opened their wallet. It spread organically — people posted screenshots of their home screens, reacted with memes, and Xverse leaned into it on the official account.

Teaser post → Icon change → Community reactions → Xverse leans in → Bull run callback
